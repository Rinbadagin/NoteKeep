Credits/Info

Written by Alchemist, starting on 10/09/2020. Based on a mockup from last year. This full rewrite is for AS 91637. This web-app is designed to keep notes locally and password-encrypted. This should be more secure than most built-in notekeeping applications (e.g. Sticky Notes or Notepad on Windows), since they are unencrypted by default.

CryptoJS library has been used for encryption and decryption of these notes. Since they are hosted on the user's computer and use private keys decided by them, these notes are fairly susceptible to brute forcing, although their security is also dependent on password complexity.
